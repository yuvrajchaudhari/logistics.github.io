CEO Message

Mr. Pravin Chaudhary-CEO

Greetings from the Virtuoso family!

“Since 2011, Virtuoso has been serving global clientele across automation, instrumentation and electrical projects and engineering services for domains such as oil and gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, power, water, sugar, infrastructure, manufacturing etc.
Virtuoso is a highly growth-oriented organization with a sturdy value system. Today, we are a team of 400+ passionate engineers who have been tirelessly working to build the success story of Virtuoso.
I strongly believe that to grow as an organization one should always envision boldly, implement strategies to achieve it and envisage the team as the core set of assets to enable this vision. I believe giving authority and free hands-on control is the biggest motivation for my team to achieve our organizational goals. I personally emphasize on creating leaders who can take ownership and uplift Virtuoso independently. I always encourage my team to keep up with this rhythm and create more leaders along with them.
Our key strength has been our loyal customers spread globally who have believed in us by providing opportunities for Virtuoso to reach new heights across a wide business spectrum. Our vendors and bankers have always been our backbone supporting us in difficult times and fueling our growth engine incessantly.
I express my sincere gratitude towards everyone who has contributed to make Virtuoso an emerging global success story. Virtuoso has come a long way and together we aim to be in the top 100 global engineering and turnkey service providers by 2035.”
